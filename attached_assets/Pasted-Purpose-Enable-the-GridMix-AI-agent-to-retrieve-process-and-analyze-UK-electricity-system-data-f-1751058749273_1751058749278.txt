 Combined Use Strategy
 Frequency:
Demand API: every 30 minutes

Generation API: once daily after all data is published (~T+1)

 Storage:
Normalize settlement periods to UTC timestamps

Store demand and generation data with consistent keys (e.g., date, fuelType, timestamp) for cross-analysis

 Use Cases Enabled in GridMix

Forecasting Inputs	Improve short-term demand/generation predictions
